Common Text Formatting Tags in HTML

  1. <strong>
    • Use cases:
      • To emphasize important text.
      • For indicating strong importance or seriousness.
    • Best practices:
      • Avoid using <strong> for purely stylistic purposes; reserve it for semantically important content.
  2. <em>
    • Use cases:
      • To emphasize text.
      • For indicating a change in tone or stress.
    • Best practices:
      • Use <em> for emphasizing text within a sentence rather than for headings or titles.
